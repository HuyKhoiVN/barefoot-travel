Essence Vietnam –7 Days

HIGHLIGHTS:

 

 

  • Experience the charm of Hanoi ’s old quarter and explore its must-see sights.
  • Discover the breathtaking natural beauty and cultural significance of Halong Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Immerse yourself in the peaceful and serene atmosphere of Hoi An, a town steeped in history and culture.
  • Visit the vibrant and bustling city of Saigon and observe the unique way of life along the Mekong River.

 

 

BRIEF ITINERARY:

 

Day 1: Ha Noi Arrival and half-day tour

Day 2: Ha Noi Ha Long Bay

Day 3: Ha Long Bay Ha Noi Flight to Da Nang Hoi An

Day 4: Hoi An Ancient Town

Day 5: Hoi An Da Nang Flight to Ho Chi Minh City

Day 6: Mekong Delta

Day 7: Ho Chi Minh and departure

 

 

DETAILED ITINERARY:

 

 

Day 1: Hanoi Arrival Half Day City Tour

 

Upon your arrival at the airport, you will be greeted by our tour guide who will transfer you to Hanoi. You will start your tour by visiting the Literature Temple, which is renowned as Vietnams first university. Then, you will enjoy a cyclo tour around the Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem Lake before attending a traditional water puppet show. You will stay overnight at the hotel.

 

 

Day 2: Hanoi Halong Bay (B, L, D)

 

You will depart for Halong Bay and, upon arrival, board a cruise and start cruising around the bay. You will witness the impressive karts with distinct formations and stop at Amazing Cave for a walk. You will also have plenty of time for kayaking, swimming, and sunbathing after the cruise anchors at a calm sea. You will enjoy a lovely sunset dinner and overnight stay on the cruise.

 

 

Day 3: Halong Bay Hanoi Flight to Da Nang Hoi An (B, L)

 

After breakfast on the cruise, you will stop at the pearl farm to see the actual cultured pearl of fishermen. Then, you will check-out from the cruise and take part in a cooking class for everyone interested in Vietnamese food. The cruise will take you further to explore the hidden beauty of the bay. You will transfer to the airport for a flight to Da Nang upon arrival back in Hanoi. Once you land, we will pick you up and transfer you to your hotel in Hoi An.

 

 

Day 4: Hoi An Ancient Town (B)

 

You will visit Hoians vibrant market and ancient port to explore the rhythm of local daily life and savor the true color, flavor, and taste of the oriental. Later, you will enjoy a walking tour to visit the Hoi An ancient town. In the afternoon, you will have free time to relax on the beach or explore more of the ancient town on your own. You will stay overnight at the hotel.

 

 

Day 4: Hoi An Ancient Town (B)

 

You will visit Hoi Ans vibrant market and ancient port to explore the rhythm of local daily life and savor the true color, flavor, and taste of the oriental. Later, you will enjoy a walking tour to visit the Hoian ancient town. In the afternoon, you will have free time to relax on the beach or explore more of the ancient town on your own. You will stay overnight at the hotel.

 

 

Day 5: Hoi An Da Nang Flight to Ho Chi Minh City (B)

 

You will have a full day tour to Bana Hills &Golden Bridge (Optional). You will have free time until we transfer you to the airport for a flight to Ho Chi Minh City (1.15 hours). We will pick you up from the airport and transfer you to the hotel for check-in.

 

 

Day 6: Mekong Delta (B, L)

 

We will transfer you to Mekong Delta, and then you will take a boat trip to explore Caibe floating market where local people sell, buy, and exchange goods on their boats. You will visit a village to watch the process of making local products, observe daily activities, enjoy Vietnamese traditional music, and take a small sampan rowing by hand to feel the real Mekong River. After lunch, you will visit a local brick kiln before taking another boat to Vinh Long, the center of tropical fruits, and stopping for a short visit at a local market.

 

 

Day 7: Ho Chi Minh Half Day City Tour Departure (B)

 

After breakfast, you will visit the War Remnants Museum, which exhibits old military equipment from the Vietnam War. You will then continue to visit the Reunification Palace, which was formerly the Presidential Palace and the site of South Vietnams surrender in April 1975. You will walk down to see Notre Dame Cathedral and the General Post Office before we transfer you to the airport for your departure flight.
